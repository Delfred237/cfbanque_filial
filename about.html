Strategic Vision

Institutional Positioning at the Heart of African Finance

Chartered Financial Investment (CHARTERED) is an African investment institution platform designed to support companies across the continent in their structural transformation, regional expansion, and integration into international financial markets.

Our positioning is grounded in a deep understanding of African economic environments, characterized by differentiated growth cycles, specific monetary constraints, evolving regulatory frameworks, and significant dependence on international capital flows.

This local expertise is combined with advanced international financial engineering capabilities, ensuring that African corporate structures align with the standards required by institutional investors, sovereign wealth funds, multilateral development banks, and global capital markets.

Global Financial Architecture

Operating Within a World-Class Financial Ecosystem

As a subsidiary of CFBANQUE INVESTMENT, CHARTERED operates within a global financial architecture framework, enabling African enterprises to access sophisticated financing mechanisms while complying with international standards such as IFRS and US GAAP.

With representation across select countries and a growing presence in West and Central Africa, Europe, and Asia via strategic partners, our specialists offer M&A advisory, equity and debt financing, structured finance, and risk management solutions.
