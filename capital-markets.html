EQUITY CAPITAL MARKETS

International ECM Advisory

CHARTERED provides comprehensive advisory services for Equity Capital Markets transactions, enabling African enterprises to diversify their financing sources and strengthen their capital base. Our interventions include strategic preparation for initial public offerings (IPOs), secondary share placements, and cross-border listings on international exchanges.

IPO Advisory

Full preparation, prospectus structuring, investor roadshows, and exchange listing coordination.

Secondary Placements

Accelerated bookbuilds, block trades, and strategic equity placements with institutional investors.

Cross-Border Listings

Dual-listings and cross-border exchanges on London, Paris, New York, and Pan-African exchanges.

Pre-IPO Structuring

Corporate reorganization, financial reporting transition, and governance framework ahead of listing.

DEBT CAPITAL MARKETS

International DCM Advisory

On the debt side, CHARTERED structures and arranges corporate bond issuances, Eurobonds, structured notes, and medium-term note programs. Our DCM team has deep expertise in pricing, documentation, and distribution to international fixed-income investors.

Eurobond Issuances

International bond structuring, documentation, and placement with global fixed-income investors.

Corporate Bond Programs

Medium-Term Note (MTN) programs and domestic bond issuances on regional exchanges.

Structured Notes

Complex structured debt instruments combining fixed-income with derivative components.

Green Bonds

ESG-aligned debt instruments targeting international sustainable finance investors.

Trade & Commodity Finance

Commodity Finance

Supply Chain Finance

Export Credit Facilities
