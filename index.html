Welcome to Chartered Financial Investment

We Work with You to Make Your Vision a Reality

Chartered Financial Investment (CHARTERED) is an African investment banking platform dedicated to supporting enterprises across the continent in their structural transformation, regional expansion, and integration into global financial markets.

As a subsidiary of CFBANQUE INVESTMENT, CHARTERED operates within a global financial architecture framework that enables African companies to access sophisticated financing mechanisms while complying with international accounting and governance standards such as IFRS and US GAAP.

Our approach combines deep knowledge of African economic environments with advanced international financial engineering capabilities. This dual expertise allows us to design financial structures aligned with the expectations of institutional investors, sovereign wealth funds, multilateral development banks, and global capital markets.
