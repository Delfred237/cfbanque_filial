Welcome to Chartered Financial Investment

We Work with You to Make Your Vision a Reality

Chartered Financial Investment (CHARTERED) is an African investment banking platform designed to support companies across the continent in their structural transformation, regional expansion, and integration into international financial markets. As a subsidiary of CFBANQUE INVESTMENT, CHARTERED operates within a global financial architecture framework, enabling African enterprises to access sophisticated financing mechanisms while complying with international standards such as IFRS and US GAAP.

Our positioning is grounded in a deep understanding of African economic environments, characterized by differentiated growth cycles, specific monetary constraints, evolving regulatory frameworks, and significant dependence on international capital flows. This local expertise is combined with advanced international financial engineering capabilities, ensuring that African corporate structures align with the standards required by institutional investors, sovereign wealth funds, multilateral development banks, and global capital markets.
