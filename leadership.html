Chairman

Laurent Bien Legbane, PhD

PhD — Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne MBA — HEC Montréal MSc Finance — ESSEC Douala Fin. Engineering — American University

Laurent Bien Legbane is an international financial executive specialising in structured finance, strategic advisory, and cross-border capital mobilisation in support of economic development and investment growth across emerging markets.

As Chairman of Chartered Financial Investment, he oversees the development and execution of strategic initiatives related to capital mobilisation, corporate finance, and cross-border transactions. As Global Chairman of CFBANQUE INVESTMENT, he defines the institution's long-term vision, drives its international expansion, and ensures alignment with global financial standards.

Prior to founding his institutions, he served as Vice President, Emerging Markets Investments at Goldman Sachs, structuring investment opportunities across high-growth markets, and as Associate Director — Risk, Francophone Region at McKinsey & Company, advising on risk management, organisational transformation, and institutional development.

Leadership Vision

Establishment of robust governance frameworks and accountability standards

Advanced financial engineering tailored to emerging market dynamics

Commitment to transparency and alignment with international standards

Sustainable investment strategies designed to generate long-term economic impact
