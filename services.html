01
Core Service

Strategic Financial Engineering

Financial engineering constitutes the technical core of our intervention. It is based on an in-depth analysis of balance sheets, cash flow structures, sectoral outlooks, and macroeconomic risks. Each transaction is designed through rigorous financial modeling incorporating cost of capital parameters, debt sustainability metrics, regulatory constraints, and long-term strategic objectives.

Our approach aims to optimize capital structure in order to reduce the weighted average cost of capital (WACC), strengthen solvency, and enhance future access to financial markets.

  • Capital Structure Optimization (WACC Reduction)
  • Senior & Mezzanine Debt Structuring
  • International Bank Syndications
  • Regional & International Bond Issuances
  • Hybrid Debt-Equity Instruments
  • Financial Risk Modeling & Stress Testing

Capital Raising & Access to International Markets

Access to capital remains a structural challenge for African enterprises. CHARTERED structures and organizes equity fundraising, international private placements, private equity transactions, and structured financing solutions. Each operation is supported by rigorous institutional documentation, including comprehensive Investment Memoranda and forward-looking financial modeling.

We serve as a strategic interface between African companies and global investors, ensuring financial communication consistent with international standards and positioning companies within a sustainable, recurring financing trajectory.

  • International Private Placements
  • Equity Capital Raising
  • Investment Memoranda Preparation
  • Private Equity Transaction Advisory
  • Cross-Border Capital Markets Access
  • Global Institutional Investor Relations

03
Strategic Advisory

Mergers, Acquisitions & Strategic Transactions

In an economic environment increasingly marked by sectoral consolidation, CHARTERED advises African enterprises in external growth operations, restructuring initiatives, and asset disposals. Mergers and acquisitions are conceived as instruments of strategic transformation enabling vertical integration, regional expansion, sectoral diversification, and enhanced competitiveness.

CHARTERED acts as an independent advisor, ensuring financial coherence, transactional transparency, and the protection of our clients' strategic interests throughout each engagement.

  • Corporate Valuation (DCF, Multiples, Sum-of-Parts)
  • Strategic Synergy Analysis
  • Financial Due Diligence
  • Transaction Structuring & Negotiation
  • Post-Merger Integration Advisory
  • Strategic Divestitures & Asset Disposals

Foundation Service

Structuring of Holdings & International Investment Vehicles

Institutional structuring represents a major strategic lever for African groups undergoing expansion. CHARTERED supports the creation of international holding companies, specialized investment vehicles, and regional structures designed to optimize governance, taxation, and risk management.

  • Specialized Investment Vehicle Structuring
  • Jurisdiction & Tax Treaty Assessment
  • Corporate Governance Framework Design

05
Sector-Specific

Infrastructure & Project Finance

Africa presents substantial infrastructure development needs. CHARTERED structures project financing solutions in sectors such as energy, transportation, telecommunications, industrial agriculture, and natural resources. Project finance is based on the analysis of future cash flows generated by the financed asset.

We design structures incorporating public-private partnerships, multilateral guarantees, and international syndications. The objective is to ensure financial viability while efficiently allocating risks among all stakeholders involved.

  • Energy & Power Infrastructure Financing
  • Transport Corridor & Logistics Finance
  • Public-Private Partnership (PPP) Advisory
  • Multilateral Development Bank Coordination
  • Blended Finance Structures
  • Long-Term Project Debt Structuring

Emerging Priority

Sustainable Finance & ESG Strategy

Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) criteria have become a decisive determinant of access to international capital. CHARTERED supports enterprises in developing ESG strategies, issuing green financial instruments, and integrating non-financial reporting standards aligned with international investor requirements.

This orientation expands the potential investor base, enhances institutional reputation, and aligns African enterprises with responsible and sustainable global development dynamics — significantly widening access to international capital markets.

  • ESG Strategy Development & Integration
  • Green Bond Structuring & Issuance
  • Climate Finance Mechanisms
  • Non-Financial Reporting Frameworks (GRI, TCFD)
  • Sustainable Infrastructure Investment Advisory
  • Impact Investor Relations

Crisis Management

Financial Restructuring & Capital Optimization

In volatile economic contexts, CHARTERED intervenes to restructure debt, renegotiate financial obligations, and optimize capital structures. Our approach is grounded in a comprehensive analysis of financial performance and sectoral outlooks, looking beyond short-term stabilization toward long-term institutional consolidation.

We design recovery and stabilization plans aimed at restoring financial stability, improving liquidity, and strengthening stakeholder confidence. Restructuring is approached as a strategic phase toward renewed institutional credibility.

  • Debt Restructuring & Renegotiation
  • Balance Sheet Optimization
  • Liquidity Recovery Planning
  • Creditor Negotiation & Mediation
  • Financial Performance Turnaround
  • Long-Term Capital Strategy Redefinition

Compliance

Governance & International Compliance

Integration into global markets requires a high standard of governance and regulatory compliance. CHARTERED supports enterprises in establishing structured boards of directors, specialized committees (audit, risk, compliance), and internal control systems aligned with international best practices.

We assist in the transition toward international accounting standards and in enhancing financial transparency. Governance is not merely a regulatory requirement — it is a decisive factor in valuation enhancement, institutional credibility, and long-term sustainability.

  • Board of Directors Structuring
  • Audit, Risk & Compliance Committees
  • IFRS & US GAAP Transition Support
  • Internal Control System Design
  • Financial Transparency Enhancement
  • Institutional Credibility Assessment
